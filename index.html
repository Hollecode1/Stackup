Stats — enter from CS2 or tracker.gg
1
Open CS2 and click your username in the top-right corner of the main menu.
2
Select Career from the dropdown menu.
3
Go to the Competitive or Premier tab. You'll see K/D, HS%, Win Rate, and match count.
4
Your Premier ELO is shown on the main menu under your name.
Note: CS2 doesn't show ADR natively. Use tracker.gg for that.
1
Go to tracker.gg/cs2 in your browser.
2
Search your Steam username or paste your Steam profile URL.
3
Your full stats are on the overview page — K/D, ADR, HS%, Win Rate all listed.
4
If your profile is private, set Steam visibility to Public first.
